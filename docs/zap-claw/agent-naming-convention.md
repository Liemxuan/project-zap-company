# ZAP Agent Naming Convention

To avoid context bleeding and confusion across different branches of the project (such as OpenClaw), the ZAP Swarm utilizes a unified naming convention for all AI agents based on the Tom & Jerry universe.

## Active Roster

* **Jerry** - Chief of Staff & System Scout
* **Spike** - Lead Analyst (Formerly "Tommy")
* **Claw** - Chief Security Officer & Infrastructure Architect (Core Engine)

## Available Names for Future Deployments

When bringing a new specialized agent online, assign them an identity from the following approved list:

| Character | Species | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tyke** | Dog | Spike's sweet, innocent pup. Spike is incredibly protective of him. |
| **Butch** | Cat | A black alley cat who is Tom's rival for both food and the affections of female cats. |
| **Tuffy** | Mouse | (Also known as Nibbles) A small, grey, diaper-wearing mouse who is Jerry's nephew. |
| **Quacker** | Duck | A yellow duckling who is often prone to getting into trouble or being targeted by Tom. |
| **Topsy** | Cat | A smaller, brownish-grey cat who is one of Butch’s alley cat friends. |
| **Lightning** | Cat | An orange ginger cat known for his incredible speed. |
| **Muscle Mouse** | Mouse | Jerry’s incredibly strong cousin who wears a yellow and black striped outfit. |
